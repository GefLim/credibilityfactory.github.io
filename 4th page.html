Dangers of
Misinformation

Medical Misinformation:

Misinformation has plagued Filipinos in medical advice where inefficient and false methods of "battling" the virus have been used. Medical misinformation is the most dangerous misinformation of all.


Fear Mongering:

The unnecessary fear created by fake news causes people to be scared of something they should not. For example, the COVID-19 vaccine, where most Filipinos feel scared of the vaccine, thinking that the vaccine would lead to an infection.


Manipulation:

An agenda usually drives misinformation, and when a person gets misinformed with this agenda in mind, they change their beliefs. This is harmful as misinformation gives a way to discredit people, organizations, and many more without checking these sources' validity; people will fall into the fake news creators' agenda to not trust these organizations anymore.
