About the Credibility Factory

The Credibility Factory is a play on the idea of an actual factory. There are regulations and procedures in a factory to regulate the quality of a product and the product is discarded when a product is regarded as not passing on quality. This is how we should be with sources where any time we see one, we investigate and verify its authenticity and if the source does not pass quality then we should not trust and share this source with others.
